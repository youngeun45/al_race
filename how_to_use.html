HOW TO USE

  1. STEP 1"Select the product"

    Choose the area you want to travel ranging from Gangbuk to Gangnam. Then, check out the nearby restaurants in the area you have chosen. You may choose 3 restaurants from that area.

  2. STEP 2"Payment"

    The price is KRW 20,000 for the Gangbuk Amazing Race; KRW 30,000 for the Gangnam Amazing Race. You can enjoy up to 50% off in the 3 restaurants you have chosen. At the retailer, pre-payment is required and you may simply pay using ‘mobile payment’ (such as ZHIFUBAO, WEIXIN PAY).You’ll receive a unique voucher once you have paid.

  3. STEP 3"Visit the store"

    Get your passport at the first store and start the Amazing Race. When ordering, present your voucher and show it to the store staff so that you may get the food you’ve ordered. Take a photo with the Amazing Race logo pose in front of the store then upload it on your social media to receive a stamp.Add more fun to your travel by earning stamps every time you visit a store.

  4. STEP 4“Claim your reward”

    You’ll receive a reward once you’ve visited your 3 chosen stores and upon collecting 3 stamps. Once it’s certified that you’ve completed the Amazing Race, you’ll be receiving coffee beans and seaweed laver as reward. (The rewards are subject to change depending on the season) Branch where you can claim rewards: Pizza Alvolo Daechi or Nipong Naepong Myeongdong Noon Square
